# Arguing-AI-Dissertation
Outlining the potential use-cases, drawbacks and middle grounds that AI proposes

This is my dissertation for Software Engineering at Solent University

I will be looking at how AI as a whole and in parts, affects us as a human race and affects the surrounding environment. 
My major points surrounding this topic are:
 - How AI is and will affect the environment
     - poisoning or limiting water sources
     - electrical consumption
     - overall emissions
     - How building datacenters / containing buildings will affect this
 - How AI is and will affect the human populus
     - The mental impact on humans
     - The "Living" impact on humans - affecting living conditions
     - The societal impact - looking at how the use of AI as of current (and projected) will affect the economy and how we navigate our daily lives
 - Cloud vs Localised AI models and how this ties in with the above
 - Specialised AI vs General AI vs LLM

My hope with this disseration is to inform the reader of the potential dangers and uses that AI is bringing and will bring in the future
and how it will affect our day to day lives so that they are able to make an educated decision on where they stand surrounding the rise or fall of AI.
